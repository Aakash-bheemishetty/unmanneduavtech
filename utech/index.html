Determine wire position

Reconstructed models allow for engineering analysis:
•Measurement of distances between wires.
•Determination of wires sag, vertical and lateral dimensions.

Agriculture

Automated UAV aerial surveying+Text(precise camera center coordinates)-> Automated data processing->Visualisation,analysis,export.
UAV payload: Camera Sony a5000/a5100,IR multispectral camera,Thermal camera,Broadband data transmission link,Gyrostabilised platform with FullHD video.
Technology Overview:When sunlight strikes a plant, certain wavelengths of spectrum are absorbed and other wavelengths are reflected. The pigment of plant leaves, chlorophyll, strongly absorbs visible light. The cell structure of the leaves, on the other hand, strongly reflects the near-infrared light. Thus, the reflectivity in these two bands are strongly relevant to crop health.

Use-cases:

Root-crops volume measuring

Root-crops volume in open storages can be easily calculated by 3D-model created on aerial surveying data. At the same time thermal surveying allows to control temperature condition and make a decision to use one or another bank in the first instance.
